# What Google Lighthouse Actually Is
 - Google Lighthouse is a free tool made by Google that checks how good your website is.
 - It automatically analyzes your site and gives scores for things like:
    - Performance – How fast your site loads ⚡
    - Accessibility – Can people with disabilities use it ♿
    - Best Practices – Are you following good web standards
    - SEO – Can search engines find your site 🔍
    - PWA – Is it installable like an app (optional)
    - It gives scores from 0–100 and tells you what to fix.

  ## Example result:

    Category	      Score
    Performance     	82
    Accessibility 	    95
    Best Practices	   100
    SEO             	90

    Then it says things like:
    "Images are too large"
    "JavaScript blocking page load"
    "Add alt text to images"
    So basically Lighthouse = automatic website tester + improvement suggestions.
